So, I'll just give you the formula first. The formula for calculating a bezier curve is! *Drum roll*(play drum roll sound) (1-t)*P0 + t(P1). Its less complicated looking than it seems huh(Just to let you know, I'm using the wonderful literary device known as foreshadowing). Let's break down this formula. First off, what's P0 and P1? They refer to coordinates. These coordinates define where our curve starts and ends. The "t" or T value refers to the percentage of the line. If say you want to get the coordinates of 50% along a line, you can use this formula to get the coordinate. Oh by the way, this formula won't yield a curve yet.
